## Director's Statement

*Fronterizos* is a deeply personal exploration of what it means to live between two cultures. Growing up in Tijuana and crossing the border for school and work, the border was never just a line on a map — it was a daily reality that shaped identity, language, and belonging.

This film seeks to capture the nuance of border life that is often reduced to headlines. The people who live this reality — the *fronterizos* — carry within them the richness of two worlds.
